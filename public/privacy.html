Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share your data with the following categories of recipients only as necessary:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who provide email delivery, hosting, or CRM services on our behalf, under strict data processing agreements.
  • Form Submission Services: We use FormSubmit (formsubmit.co) to relay demo request emails. Your submission data is transmitted to their servers solely for email delivery.
  • Legal Authorities: Where required by applicable law, court order, or government authority in India.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the successor entity, subject to the same protections.
07

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes set out in this policy:

  • Demo / Enquiry Data: Up to 2 years from the date of your last interaction with us, unless you request earlier deletion.
  • Server Logs & Usage Data: Up to 90 days, after which they are anonymised or deleted.
  • Legal / Compliance Records: As required by applicable Indian law (typically 7 years for accounting records).

When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

08

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure, including:

  • HTTPS / TLS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorised personnel only.
  • Regular review of data handling practices.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please notify us immediately at info@edvieye.com if you suspect any breach.

How to Delete Your Account / Data

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data held by Edvieye at any time. Follow the steps below:

1
Send a deletion request email
Email us at info@edvieye.com with the subject line: "Account / Data Deletion Request". Include your full name, email address, and phone number so we can locate your records.
2
Identity verification
We may ask you to confirm your identity to prevent unauthorised deletion requests. This is typically done by verifying the email address or phone number associated with your data.
3
We process your request
We will process your deletion request within 30 days. Your personal data (name, email, phone, organisation) will be permanently deleted from our systems and any third-party processors we have shared it with, except where retention is required by law.
4
Confirmation sent to you
Once deletion is complete, we will send you a confirmation email. If your request cannot be fully fulfilled (e.g., due to a legal retention obligation), we will explain the reason and the data that must be retained.
ℹ️
Note: If you are a school or institution using the Edvieye ERP platform, account deletion for student and staff records is managed by your institution's administrator through the platform dashboard. For platform-level data deletion, please contact your school's designated Edvieye administrator or reach us at info@edvieye.com.
09

Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and applicable Indian privacy law, you have the following rights in respect of your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request confirmation of whether we process your data and obtain a copy.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data where there is no overriding legal reason for us to continue processing it.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Lodge a complaint with us or with the Data Protection Board of India.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at info@edvieye.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days. We may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request.
